Evolutional Valchromat Tables Solution

Valchromat is a panel made of wood fibres coloured in the production process. The fibres are impregnated with organic colouring agents and chemically bound to one another by a special resin which lends Valchromat unique physicochemical features, allows exploring the third dimension, the beauty of textures and the best of engineering. Valchromat added value of comes not only from color, but from the distinct composition.
